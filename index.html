About Me

I am a Ph.D. student with the School of Software Engineering at South China University of Technology. I also received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from the same university in 2018. My research interests include computer vision and AutoML. I served as the reviewer for several top-tier conferences, including ICML, NeurIPS, CVPR and ECCV.

Open-source Projects (Selected)

  • chenyaofo/image-classification-codebase : A codebase for the image classification task implemented by PyTorch, which does not use any high-level deep learning libraries (such as pytorch-lightening or MMClassification) and thus is easy to follow and modified.

  • chenyaofo/pytorch-cifar-models : A model zoo consisted of pretrained classified models on CIFAR-10/100 in PyTorch, including vgg, resnet, mobilenetv2, shufflenetv2 and so on.

  • chenyaofo/deep-learning-model-deploy-tutorial : A tutorial for deep learning model deployment, which contains TensorRT, ONNX, PPLNN and OpenVINO frameworks. It also includes a performance benchmark on varying hardwares with above deployment frameworks.

  • chenyaofo/statistical-learning-method-numpy-impl : Numpy implementations for algorithms in the book Statistical Learning Method (by Li Hang), which is highly efficient and can be directly runing on Colab without cloning the codes to the local.

  • chenyaofo/awesome-vision-architecture : An up-to-date list of progress made in deep learning vision architectures, which includes but not limited to papers (backbone design, loss deisgn, tricks etc), datasets, codebases, frameworks and etc.

  • chenyaofo/awesome-architecture-search : An up-to-date list of progress made in neural architecture search, which includes but not limited to papers, datasets, codebases, frameworks and etc.
